Why should I donate blood ?

Every ten minutes someone in Kenya needs a blood transfusion. Without our voluntary blood donations we cannot demand blood from hospitals.



Your donation makes a real difference and can save several lives for example, as little as three teaspoonfuls of your blood can save the life of a premature baby.
